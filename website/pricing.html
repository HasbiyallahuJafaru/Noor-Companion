FAQ

Common questions

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Yes. You can cancel Premium at any time from the app. Your subscription remains active until the end of the billing period — you won't be charged again after cancelling.

How are therapists verified?

Every therapist on Noor Companion is reviewed and approved by our admin team before they appear in the directory. We check qualifications, experience, and suitability.

Is the payment secure?

Yes. All payments are processed by Paystack, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. We never store your card details — Paystack handles everything securely.

Do I need to book a call in advance?

No. Premium subscribers can call any available therapist instantly — no booking or scheduling required. If a therapist doesn't answer within 60 seconds, you can try another.

What happens if a therapist isn't available?

If a therapist doesn't join within 60 seconds, the call is marked as missed and you're notified. You can immediately try another therapist in the directory.

Is the free plan really free forever?

Yes. The Free plan is free forever with no time limit. You only need Premium if you want to call therapists. All Islamic content is fully accessible on Free.
