Built for recovery

Your complete toolkit for the journey back

Every Islamic practice, habit, and connection you need to overcome your desires and rebuild your life — one day at a time.

Daily Dhikr

Replace the urge with remembrance. Morning and evening adhkar with Arabic text, audio, and a tasbih counter — keeping your mind anchored to Allah when it needs it most.

Quran Recitations

Let the Quran calm your nafs. All 114 surahs with Arabic, translation, and high-quality audio — your companion through every difficult hour, day or night.

Prayer Times

Structure is the foundation of recovery. Five daily prayers become five anchors in your day — accurate salah times, GPS-based, with a live countdown to refocus your mind.

Streak Tracking

Celebrate every day you stayed strong. Your streak grows with every day you engage your deen. Milestone rewards at 7, 14, 30, and 100 days remind you how far you've come — and how much you're worth.
